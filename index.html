02The insight

Humans are not text.

/01

Before language

LLMs predict behaviour from text. The decisions that matter most happen in the first 200 milliseconds — before reason has a chance to intervene.

/02

Everyone builds System 2

Every major lab is building slow, deliberate reasoning. No one is building System 1 — the instinctive layer that decides who holds the line and who breaks.

/03

Billions spent blind

Militaries, security forces and pro sports spend billions training for the decisive moment — with no way to measure what actually happens inside it.

Pressure reveals everyone eventually. Rankacy AI sees it first.
